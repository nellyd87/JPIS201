Skills

Tools, training, and competencies.

Through my varied experience in the Parks and Recreation field, I've gathered a wide range of skills I can draw from. While not all are listed below, I would like to spotlight a few examples.

Over the course of the past five years, my department has utilized a project management tool called Basecamp. This tool helps our teams, both internal and external, coordinate, communicate, and stay organized in real time

The City of South Jordan has a strong focus on risk mangement and emergency preparedness. As a result of this focus, I have been trained in FEMA ICS courses and implement ICS protocols in our Recreation Department's planned special events. While doing so can take some extra time and effort, the opportunity to train and practice these protocols within our teams has been a great benefit and keeps us all sharp and prepared for emergency incidents.
