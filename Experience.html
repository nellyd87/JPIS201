About Me

This is what I do.

Through the years I have been very fortunate to have had such great work opportunities within the Parks and Recreation industry. When I was 15 years old I became lifeguard certified and was thrilled with any opportunity to learn new skills and postions. Because of this, I held just about every position in exsistance within the aquatics management field.

Building on the foundation I'd built in the aquatics realm, I moved up into broader recreation facility and program management. In Daybreak, a large master planned community, I was able to provide services to the community through management of a recreation center, clubhouse, multiple swimming pools, splash pads, parks, pavilions, and a unique man-made lake with associated activies and wildlife.

Currently I serve the residents of the City of South Jordan as the Recreation Department Director. Through this position I'm able to support my team members through developing clear and meaningful department direction, ensuring they have the tools and resources they need to succeed. While this role has been challenging and stretched me as a leader, it has by far been the most rewarding and I love seeing the difference my work and efforts make in my team's work and ultimately the services we provide to the community.
